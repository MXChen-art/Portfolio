About Me（关于）

Liu Yuankun's shop was located opposite Fengshun Middle School, with a faded wooden sign hanging on the doorframe, inscribed with the four regular script characters 'Yuankun Rice Noodle Soup,' written by himself. This year, he was twenty-two years old, and he had been running this small shop of less than ten square meters for a full ten years. Teachers, students from Fengshun Middle School, and neighbors almost all knew Liu Yuankun. People privately said he was a 'shrewd businessman.' While others charged only ten yuan for a bowl of rice noodle soup, he demanded fifty. Ten years ago, Liu Yuankun had just turned twelve. His brows and eyes still bore a trace of childlikeness, yet in his heart had already planted a firm thought—he wanted to open a rice noodle soup shop of his own. Some advised him to give up this broken shop and instead work in a factory. Liu Yuankun just shook his head stubbornly, saying, 'I must make it big and strong, and achieve greatness again.' Day by day passed, and Liu Yuankun still stuck to his rice noodle soup shop, opening early every day and closing late at night. But business was worsening by the day. Liu Yuankun picked up the tools in his hands, gently wiping them, his eyes revealing a trace of sadness: 'I really regret it, I really should have gone to the factory earlier.' He sat at the shop entrance in the evening. The sunset shone through the eaves, falling on Liu Yuankun and also on the faded wooden sign. Finally, his rice noodle soup shop closed down.

刘源坤的铺子开在丰顺中学对面，一块褪了色的木牌挂在门框上，刻着“源坤粄汤”四个楷体字，是他亲手写的。今年他二十二岁，守着这家不足十平米的小店，已经整整十年了。 丰顺中学的师生和周边街坊，几乎都认识刘源坤，人们私下里都说他是个“奸商”。别人一碗粄汤只卖十块钱，他却要收五十。 十年前，刘源坤刚满十二岁，眉眼间还带着未脱的稚气，心底却已埋下了一个坚定的念头——他要开一家属于自己的粄汤店。 有人劝他，别守着这破店了，不如进厂打工。刘源坤只是摇摇头，倔强的说：“我一定要做大做强，再创辉煌。” 日子一天天过去，刘源坤依旧守着他的粄汤店，每天早早开门，很晚才关门。但是生意却是一天不如一天了。 刘源坤拿起手里的工具，轻轻擦拭着，眼神透露出一丝悲凉：“真后悔，我真不如早点进厂。” 他坐在铺子门口，傍晚时分，夕阳透过屋檐，洒在刘源坤的身上，也洒在那块褪色的木牌上，终于他的粄汤店倒闭了。
